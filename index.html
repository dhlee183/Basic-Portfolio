About Me

I was born in South Korea and came to New Jersey when I was 3 years old. I grew up in New Jersey and went to college at Rutgers University with a major in pharmacy. But I decided to change majors and change schools so I transferred to Eastern Michigan University and graduated with a degree in business administration with a concentration in accounting.
